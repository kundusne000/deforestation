Deforestation

Plus ways you can help!

Did you know deforestation accounts for nearly 24% of all greenhouse gas emissions? Greenhouse gas emissions can lead to far-ranging environmental and health effects. Extreme weather is one of many detrimental effects of climate change which is caused by greenhouse gas emissions. But how can we help stop such a large crisis? If everyone in America recycled just one plastic bottle, those materials could make more than 54 million T-shirts!
